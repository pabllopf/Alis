◆ Attenuation

float Alis.Core.Audio.SFML.SoundStream.Attenuation
getset

Attenuation factor of the stream. The attenuation is a multiplicative factor which makes the music more or less loud according to its distance from the listener. An attenuation of 0 will produce a non-attenuated sound, i.e. its volume will always be the same whether it is heard from near or from far.
